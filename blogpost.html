An Adult's perspective on Technology

In this interview, I spoke to a teacher that goes to my school. This teacher; Adam, teaches physics and enjoys "messing around" with technology.

Me: How old were you when you got your first computer

Adam: 17, in like 2007

Me: What was your favorite piece of technology when you were growing up?

Adam: The playstation portable , because it had hacks and custom things, it was the coolest thing.

Me: How has technology changed over your lifetime?

Adam: Now it's more user friendly, but less flexable, like you can only do what apple says you can do. It's generally good , technology is more accessable"

Me: What is your greatest hope for technology in the future?

Adam: To become more like responsible , nowadays technology is trying to take advange of human phychology to use their apps.

Me: What is your greatest fear for technology in the future?

Adam: Well,technology turns you into total zombies.

Me:What advice would you like to share with the younger generation about technology?

Adam: Don't be afraid to mess with technology , get out of your confort zone, try different sysyems.

Me: What do you think is the coolest technology at the moment?

Adam: Space flight because so much technology has come through space programming , communication is possible bc of satallite.
